Meet the Cafe Delight Team

Shivam Semwal – Founder & Visionary

Shivam is the heart and soul behind Cafe Delight. With a deep passion for hospitality and a love for bringing people together, Shivam envisioned a space where community, creativity, and great food converge. As the founder, he is involved in every aspect of the cafe, from the daily operations to curating the perfect blend of coffee and cuisine.

Shivam’s expertise in business management and hospitality shines through the warm and inviting atmosphere of the cafe. His dedication to maintaining high standards of quality and service has made Cafe Delight a beloved spot for both locals and visitors. Shivam is committed to ensuring every guest feels at home, whether they’re enjoying a solo coffee break or celebrating a special occasion with loved ones.

Rudra – Head Chef

Rudra is the culinary genius behind the scenes at Cafe Delight. With years of experience in the kitchen and a deep love for food, Rudra creates the dishes and drinks that make Cafe Delight unique. His passion for culinary arts is reflected in every dish that leaves the kitchen – from the rich, aromatic masala chai to the flavorful Tandoori Mocha.

Rudra is constantly experimenting with flavors, textures, and presentation, ensuring that each dish is not only delicious but also a work of art. His meticulous approach to sourcing fresh ingredients and his commitment to sustainability are key to the cafe’s success. Whether it’s a classic dish or an innovative fusion creation, Rudra’s dishes are a true reflection of his creativity and expertise.

Kavita – Line Cook & Prep Cook

Kavita is the backbone of our kitchen, ensuring that every dish is prepared with precision and care. As a line cook and prep cook, Kavita is responsible for maintaining the high standards of quality and consistency that Cafe Delight is known for. Her attention to detail and ability to work efficiently under pressure make her an invaluable member of the team.

Her skill in preparing everything from delicate pastries to hearty dishes helps keep the kitchen running smoothly. Kavita’s dedication to her craft shines through in the dishes she prepares, always with a focus on fresh, flavorful ingredients. Her calm demeanor and teamwork make her a crucial part of ensuring that the cafe’s food is always delicious and ready to serve.

Jigu – Kitchen Helper

Jigu plays a vital role in supporting the kitchen team at Cafe Delight. As a kitchen helper, she is responsible for assisting with food prep, cleaning, and ensuring that the kitchen remains organized and efficient. Jigu’s hard work and dedication help the chefs focus on creating delicious dishes while she manages the essential tasks that keep everything running smoothly.

With her attention to detail and a strong work ethic, Jigu ensures that the kitchen operates at peak performance, from maintaining a clean workspace to organizing ingredients. Her friendly and positive attitude makes her an invaluable team member who contributes greatly to the success of the cafe.

Aishwarya - Beverage Innovator

Aishwaryais the mastermind behind Café Delight’s extraordinary beverage menu, bringing a blend of creativity and precision to every drink. With an innate ability to balance flavors and an unrelenting passion for innovation, she crafts beverages that go beyond the ordinary. Whether it's developing a unique seasonal latte or introducing bold new blends to the menu, Aishwarya’s expertise ensures that every sip is a sensory experience. Her meticulous attention to detail, from selecting premium beans and leaves to experimenting with cutting-edge brewing techniques, has earned her recognition as a trailblazer in the café industry.

Her love for beverages extends to understanding the stories and cultures behind every ingredient she works with. Aishwarya frequently collaborates with local farmers and suppliers to ensure sustainability and quality in her creations. Beyond crafting drinks, she also dedicates herself to training the barista team, sharing her knowledge and inspiring others to see beverages as a form of art. Under her leadership, Café Delight continues to be a destination where guests can savor both tradition and innovation in every cup.

Teertha - Culinary Artist

Teerthais the creative soul of Café Delight’s kitchen, transforming simple ingredients into masterpieces that tantalize the senses. Her culinary journey is fueled by a deep respect for global cuisines and a talent for infusing traditional dishes with contemporary flair. Each dish she creates tells a story, blending textures, flavors, and presentation to create a harmonious dining experience. Her menu is a testament to her expertise, with signature dishes like the Paneer Tikka Quesadilla and Tandoori Chicken Sliders showcasing her ability to seamlessly merge cultural influences into unforgettable flavors.

Beyond her skills as a chef, Teertha is a passionate advocate for sourcing fresh, high-quality ingredients and incorporating sustainable practices into her kitchen. She believes that food should not only nourish but also create meaningful connections, and she works tirelessly to make every dish a celebration of flavor and craftsmanship. Teertha’s dedication, creativity, and leadership have made her an indispensable part of Café Delight, ensuring that every guest leaves with a lasting impression of culinary excellence.

Anshuman & Saliha – Front-of-House Staff

Anshuman and Saliha are the friendly faces you’ll see greeting you when you walk into Cafe Delight. As front-of-house staff, they are responsible for creating a welcoming atmosphere and ensuring that every guest feels valued. Whether they’re taking your order, offering recommendations, or delivering your food with a smile, Anshuman and Saliha go above and beyond to make every dining experience memorable.

Both Anshuman and Saliha bring a warm energy to the cafe, creating a friendly and approachable environment for guests. They have a deep knowledge of the menu, ensuring that customers get the perfect meal or drink tailored to their preferences. Their passion for excellent customer service and their positive attitude make them a big part of why guests keep coming back.

His organizational skills and attention to detail help maintain the seamless operation of the cafe.

In addition to his accounting duties, Sandes also plays a crucial role in assisting in the kitchen. His ability to multitask, whether it’s managing budgets or helping with food preparation, makes him an indispensable part of the team. Sandes’s commitment to excellence ensures that Cafe Delight’s operations remain smooth, allowing the entire team to focus on delivering exceptional experiences to customers.

Our Team's Philosophy

The team at Cafe Delight is more than just a group of employees – we are a family. We share a love for great food, excellent service, and creating a space where everyone feels welcome. Each member brings their unique skills, passion, and creativity to the table, and together we work hard to make Cafe Delight a place where guests feel like they’re part of something special.

We believe in working together to create memorable experiences for our customers. Whether it’s through the food we prepare, the drinks we serve, or the warm atmosphere we provide, every team member contributes to the overall experience that makes Cafe Delight a beloved destination.
